# Founder's Cabinet — Vercel Deployment Guide

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  TOTAL TIME: 10 MINUTES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

You'll have a live URL anyone can visit at the end.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 1: SIGN UP FOR VERCEL (2 minutes)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to vercel.com
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Choose "Continue with GitHub" (or Google/email)
4. Confirm your email if needed
5. You're in.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 2: DEPLOY THE APP (3 minutes)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Two options — pick the easier one for you:

━━━ OPTION A: DRAG AND DROP (EASIEST) ━━━

1. On the Vercel dashboard, look for "Add New..." → "Project"
2. Or go to: vercel.com/new
3. Look for the option that says "Deploy without Git" 
   OR scroll down to find "Browse all templates" 
   OR look for the upload area
4. Drag the entire "vercel" folder onto the page
5. Vercel will detect it as a static site
6. Click "Deploy"

━━━ OPTION B: CLI DEPLOYMENT (IF DRAG-DROP DOESN'T WORK) ━━━

If you have Terminal/Command Prompt:

1. Install Vercel CLI:
   npm install -g vercel

2. Open Terminal in the "vercel" folder:
   cd path/to/vercel

3. Run:
   vercel

4. It will ask:
   - "Set up and deploy?" → Y
   - "Which scope?" → (pick your account)
   - "Link to existing project?" → N
   - "Project name?" → founders-cabinet
   - "Directory?" → ./
   - "Override settings?" → N

5. Wait 30 seconds. You'll get a URL.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 3: ADD YOUR API KEY (3 minutes — CRITICAL)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This is the most important step. Without this, the AI 
features won't work.

1. In Vercel dashboard, click on your "founders-cabinet" project
2. Click "Settings" (top tab)
3. Click "Environment Variables" in the left sidebar
4. Click "Add New" or "+ Add"
5. Fill in:
   - KEY:   ANTHROPIC_API_KEY
   - VALUE: (paste your key from console.anthropic.com)
            Starts with sk-ant-...
   - ENVIRONMENT: ✓ Production ✓ Preview ✓ Development (check all)
6. Click "Save"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 4: REDEPLOY TO ACTIVATE THE KEY (1 minute)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The API key only takes effect after a redeploy:

1. In your project dashboard, click "Deployments" (top tab)
2. Find the most recent deployment at the top
3. Click the "..." menu (three dots) on the right
4. Click "Redeploy"
5. Confirm "Redeploy"
6. Wait 30-60 seconds

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 5: VISIT YOUR LIVE APP (1 minute)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Click "Visit" or copy the URL at the top of your project
2. You'll see something like:
   founders-cabinet-yourname.vercel.app
3. Open it in your browser
4. Test the onboarding flow
5. Try "Analyze My Idea" — the AI should respond!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  TROUBLESHOOTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

▸ "API key not configured" error
  → Go back to Step 3. Make sure the key name is EXACTLY
    ANTHROPIC_API_KEY (all caps, underscore)
  → Then do Step 4 (redeploy) — env vars only activate
    after redeploy

▸ White screen / app doesn't load
  → Open browser console (F12 or right-click → Inspect)
  → Look for errors
  → Most common: index.html didn't upload correctly
  → Try the deployment again

▸ "Invalid response format" error  
  → This means the request didn't reach Anthropic
  → Check Vercel function logs:
    Project → Deployments → click latest → Functions tab
  → Look for errors

▸ AI works on first try then fails
  → You may have hit your Anthropic credit limit
  → Add more credit at console.anthropic.com → Billing
